Context

Sets the stage for everything that follows. This chapter answers: what is JOSYN, what does it replace, who are its stakeholders, and what does it connect to? It establishes the identity and purpose of the system before any technical or organisational detail is introduced.

In Scope

  • JOSYN's purpose and high-level identity
  • The predecessor system (JobSystem) — what remains, what is overcome
  • Stakeholders and user roles
  • External systems and integrations at a high level

Out of Scope

  • How JOSYN is internally designed → see architecture/
  • How to deploy or operate it → see 05-deployment-and-operations/
  • Company governance and politics → see 06-company-fit/
