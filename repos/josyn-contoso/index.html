josyn-contoso

Demo/placeholder company adapter repository for the JOSYN platform.

Implements IConfigSource from JOSYN.Backend.AdapterContracts with hardcoded fake values to demonstrate the ADR-009 adapter pattern end-to-end.

In a real deployment, the company would maintain their own equivalent of this repo — JOSYN has no knowledge of its existence.
